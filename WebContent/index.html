BioSearch

A Semantic Search Engine for the Life Science Linked Data

How to use:
  • Press <SPACE> to convert your text to a case-insensitive keyword, e.g. penicillin
  • Faceted filters begin with C: for classes, P: for properties and S: for datasets, e.g. C:drug P:label S:kegg
  • Recommend to use Google Chrome. For bug report or information inquiry, please click here.
